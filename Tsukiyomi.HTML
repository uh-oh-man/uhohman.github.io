COMMISSION RULES:

-Please remember art takes time so it can easily range from a day to maybe a week or so depending on what's going on to receive the art piece.
-I will require half payment up front then half after receiving the art. Refunds will not be available since this is an online purchase and I have no idea if you keep the piece or not.
-I hold the right to say ok to or decline any request, I will probably explain if needed but I'm drawing the art and if I'm not ok with drawing it then it goes.
-All payments will go through Cash app, Zelle or Paypal
-By requesting a commission you are agreeing that you will only use the illustration for personal uses and will not resell it and claim it as your own work.
-You may post the art if you'd like but please remember to give credit if you do
-My art pieces will be watermarked as they aren't yours to claim
-If you are found violating any of my rules or anything said you may not be able to do via DM's then you will be located and blacklisted, you will no longer be able to purchase a commission from me again.
-You are required to provide me with a reference image (more than one is completely fine!), this is a very important step for me to accurately visualize and create your request. This is more specifically required for detailed and complex designs and very specific poses, and to avoid unnecessary messages back and forth. If you have no idea for the pose then you can inform me on that but otherwise I need to know how to draw the said character.
-I will show you the sketch of the character to make sure everything you'd like is correctly done, after given the go to then I will finish it up
-If you would like updates of how its coming out then I will be able to provide the sketch first, line art, flat color and then anything after that will be considered finished
-If I'm not ok with something being drawn by my hands then I will mention that and we can make a work around, that being said please keep in mind I'm an open artist and will be mostly ok with drawing a lot of things like gore or "special" work but if I do tell you I'm not ok will giving you that then please understand that.
-By requesting you are agreeing to have read and accepted all terms and conditions listed above.
